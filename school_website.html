Admissions are open for Nursery to Class XII. Take the first step towards a bright future.

01

Submit Application

Fill in the online or offline registration form with all required personal and academic details of the student.

02

Entrance Assessment

Appear for a grade-appropriate assessment (Classes I–XII). Nursery–KG admissions are interaction-based.

Submit transfer certificate, previous marksheets, birth certificate, address proof, and passport photos.

World-Class Curriculum

Affiliated to HBSE, our curriculum blends rigorous academics with practical learning experiences across all levels.

🌸

Pre-Primary

Playful, activity-based learning for Nursery, LKG & UKG to build a strong foundation.
